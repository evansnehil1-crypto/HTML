Facts About Cats

Cats sleep for about 12–16 hours a day. They are very agile and can jump up to six times their body length. Cats also use their whiskers to sense their surroundings and navigate in the dark.

Studies show that owning a cat can reduce stress and anxiety. The sound of a cat’s purr can even help people relax and feel calmer!
